Symbiotica
Brand identity and digital experience
Symbiotica is a premium wellness brand that creates science-backed nutritional supplements. The brand identity system explores the intersection of nature and technology, using organic forms paired with precise typography to communicate both efficacy and elegance. The digital experience emphasizes education and transparency, allowing customers to understand the science behind each product.
